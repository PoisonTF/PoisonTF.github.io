Poisoned Item Scanner

Scan your Inventory for Poisoned Items and Protect your Account

What are Poisoned Items?

  • Poisoned Items are In-Game items that have been created or generated via a Glitch, Exploit or Fraud
  • These items can often result in your Steam Account being Permanently Trade-Banned!
  • As a Service to the TF2 Community, Backpack.tf now offers a Dedicated Poisoned Item Scanner for Free!
  • Just Press the "Send a Trade Offer" Button below to have a bot check your items!
  • Poisoned Items Scanner

Poisoned Item Scanning Service

Please Read Carefully Before Sending A Trade

The following items are cannot be Scanned due to issues with how their histories are stored on Valve Servers:

  • Halloween Spells
  • Crates
  • Unique Craft # Weapons
  • Untradeable Items
  • Unmarketable Items
  • Chroma 2 Case Key
  • Chroma 3 Case Key
  • Gamma Case Key
  • Gamma 2 Case Key
  • Operation Phoenix Case Key
  • Operation Breakout Case Key
  • Operation Wildfire Case Key
  • Operation Vanguard Case Key

When sending an offer to our trade bots, make sure that you send every tradeable item from your inventory, the bot can only provide an accurate reading for which items may be Poisoned or not if the bot is able to scan all of your items, after you have sent your items, the bot will send you a message via Steam and take 5 minutes to run checks on Item Histories and Flags, afterwards you will recieve a message containing the names of any poisoned items and a trade with your original backpack returned.

If your backpack contains any Poisoned items we recommend you contact steam support and ask them to investigate the source you recieved this poisoned item from, they should usually replace the item with a new clean history item which will remove the poisoned status from the item, please do not trade your items to another player as this may endanger their account

Important: Your offer may take a while to process during Steam downtime. Use your Sent Trade Offers page to track progress.

At backpack.tf, one of the things we do is parse inventories and keep a record of every item we come across. This allows us to keep track of item history and produce the statistics that are presented throughout the site.

With our large range of data, we are able to track down and alert users of Poisoned items that have been found in their player inventories in order to protect their account.

Many players have been Permanently Trade-Banned from accepting Poisoned Items, which make their accounts look fraudulent to Steam Support and this regularly results in a Permanent Trade Ban on the account.

Our service is Free of Charge to users of Backpack.tf, if you have any issues regarding the bot please feel free to contact us via our Discord Server.
